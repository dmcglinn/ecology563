This is a course in statistical modeling for ecologists. Topics include the following.

  • Basic concepts in regression
  • Standard statistical designs for experiments: analysis of variance, analysis of covariance, and split plot designs
  • Bayesian approach to statistics
  • Mixed effects models: random intercepts and slopes models, multilevel models with 2 and 3 levels, hierarchical Bayesian modeling, nonlinear mixed effects models, models with nested and crossed random effects
  • Maximum likelihood estimation
  • Analysis of binary and grouped binary data
  • Analysis of count data
